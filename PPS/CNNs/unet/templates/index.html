About CEUNIM

The University Center for Medical Imaging (CEUNIM) is an institution focused on deepening the connection between the academic and healthcare sectors in the field of complex multimodal diagnostic imaging and public health and education policies.

It represents the first experience in which a national university undertakes, under its sole responsibility, a project of this nature. CEUNIM is affiliated with the School of Science and Technology at the National University of San Martín, where the strengths of its areas in medical physics and biomedical engineering provide an ideal environment for achieving the center's objectives.

The Center aims to introduce new techniques for the acquisition, processing, and quantification of clinical studies into the national medical research space. This endeavor is made possible by the multidisciplinary concentration of faculty researchers with backgrounds in medical and physical-mathematical fields.
